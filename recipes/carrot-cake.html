Carrot Cake with Chocolate

Description:

Brazilian carrot cake is nicely moist and fluffy, smells like a sunny morning in spring, has a beautiful bright dough, and the chocolate glaze is a sweet finishing touch.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups (300 grams) carrots, peeled
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 ½ cup (325 grams) sugar
  • 1 cup (250 milliliters) vegetable oil
  • 2 cups (256 grams) all-purpose flour, sifted
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • A pinch of salt

Chocolate Sauce

You can use a brigadeiro glaze to cover your cake! For that, use half of the quantities of the brigadeiro recipe.

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven at 350°F/180°C.
  2. Butter the springform pan and dust flour through it.
  3. In a large bowl, add the baking powder to the flour and mix the ingredients delicately. Set aside.
  4. Add the eggs, oil, salt, sugar, and peeled carrots cut in chunks to the blender and blend until it’s homogeneous.
  5. Pour the mixture into the large bowl with flour and mix gently until all flour has incorporated.
  6. Pour the batter into the springform pan and bake it for approximately 50 minutes.
  7. Once you’re done, remove the cake from the oven and let it cool down for 10-15 minutes before removing the springform pan.
  8. Follow the brigadeiro recipe for making the chocolate glaze (you don't need to roll them, stop before this step).
  9. Remove the springform pan and pour the chocolate sauce onto the cake.
  10. Serve it with a cup of coffee and enjoy!
